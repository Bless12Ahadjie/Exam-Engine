Exam Mode Control Rule

  • Do not use the microphone and webcam to monitor the examinee throughout the duration of the exam
  • Students must show a photo ID before starting the exam.
  • Uses human proctors to monitor students via webcams in real-time.
  • Records the exam session for later review by proctors.
  • Prevent students from accessing other applications, websites, or copying/pasting during the exam.
  • Track and restrict browser activities to ensure compliance
